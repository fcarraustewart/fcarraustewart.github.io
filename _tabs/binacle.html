A binacle is the housing that holds a ship's compass — this one helps you navigate between tech stacks and the dev blogs that use them. Big red nodes are areas (categories), blue nodes are stacks (tags), and purple nodes are posts. Click a post to open it; click a stack or area to focus its neighbourhood. It grows automatically as new posts are tagged.
